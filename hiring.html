Hiring

Here at Outback Steakhouse, you would be surprised to learn that we are very selective about our employees.
Employees are required to have at least two years experience hunting small game and demonic entities, and are recommended to have basic knowledge of Arcane rituals.
Working at Outback Steakhouse comes with great benefits, including a 401k, easy access to forbidden texts on necromancy, and a 35% discount on food.
However, you will be paid entirely in human souls.

If you're lucky enough to be chosen for this apprenticeship job, then you will have to partake in an eldritch ritual requiring that you pledge the life of your firstborn child to Cthulu.
Your uniform will not be paid for.
